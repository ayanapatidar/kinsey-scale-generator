what's a kinsey scale?

the kinsey scale was proposed by alfred kinsey in 1948 to define sexuality on a continuous scale instead of a binary. this structure has, in popular internet culture, been extrapolated to other perceieved dichotomies, like "cat person vs. dog person", for example. here is how the original kinsey scale was interpreted:
